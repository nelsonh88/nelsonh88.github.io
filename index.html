About Me

I am a full stack web developer who is motivated, driven, and a compassionate individual that will continue to grow in the web development field. In January 2018 I made a career change from the physical therapy/ health field to the web development field. After months of recommendations from friends and along with my own research in the field I decided to jump right into the field and enroll into a web development immersive program at General Assembly. I had absolutely no prior experience in the web development field when I enrolled into the 3-month immersive program at General Assembly. I love the freedom of the field to explore different avenues in web development and that it supports my entrepreneurial drive. I would love to work in a company that allows me to grow and learn as a full stack web developer and eventually become a leader in the field! I am excited for this new chapter of my life and to see where my web development journey will take me. Are you ready to be part of this journey with me?
